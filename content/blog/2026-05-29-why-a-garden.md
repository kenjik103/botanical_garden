Title: Why a garden?
Date: 2026-05-29 10:00
Slug: why-a-garden

A website doesn't have to be a résumé. This one is a garden: a place to plant
small things, let them grow, and not worry too much about whether they add up
to a coherent product.

The structure reflects that. The blog and gallery are the tended beds — uniform,
predictable, easy to add to. The **projects** section is the wild corner: each
one is its own little walled world, free to look and behave however it wants
without disturbing anything else.

The plan is to keep planting.
