Title: On the aesthetic
Date: 2026-05-30 18:00
Slug: on-the-aesthetic

The look here is **Frutiger Aero** by way of Y2K and Winamp: glossy window
chrome, beveled edges, sky-and-aqua gradients, the optimistic glassy sheen of
mid-2000s software. It's a deliberately dated future — the one we were promised
around the time everything had a reflection and a soft glow.

Worth saying plainly: the "Winamp" reference is *visual only*. There's no audio
player here, no media controls, nothing that plays. Just the chrome.

Every color and gradient runs through CSS custom properties, so the whole site
can change its skin by swapping one set of variables. More skins to come.
